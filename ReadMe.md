Description
This is a detailed description about, my education background, interest , my recent projects and lastly my job experience.

 The exhibit is about farmer website that helps them to sell their produce directly to buyers. It’s a platform which offers them chance to showcase varieties of farm produce.
Our system focuses on direct to consumer marketing strategy. The strategy will help in local food distribution. The direct to consumer means all middle men are cut off the food distribution. Our system offers farmers a communal space in which multiple farmers gather to sale their produce directly to consumers via our platform.
